AI for Smarter Law

At IIT Kharagpur, we combine the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to develop tools to assist Law practitioners and the common person with legal needs. From summarizing judgments and translating legal texts to rhetorical role labelling, statute identification and intelligent precedent search, our solutions make Law more accessible, efficient and transparent.

Our Expertise

Our mission is to bridge the gap between Law and Technology, making legal processes smarter, faster, accessible and more transparent.

Legal Document Summarization

Turning lengthy judgments and statutes into concise summaries.

Legal Text Translation

Making legal language more understandable for the Indian population.

Rhetorical Role Labelling

Identifying the semantic segments in a judgement for structured analysis.

Statute Identification

Automatically detecting and linking relevant statutory provisions.

Legal Question Answering

Generating accurate responses to legal queries.

Case Law Retrieval

Intelligent and fast access to relevant precedents.
