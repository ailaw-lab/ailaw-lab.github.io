AI for Smarter Law

At IIT Kharagpur, we combine the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to transform the legal domain. From summarizing judgments and simplifying complex texts to rhetorical role labelling, statute identification, intelligent search, and predictive analytics, our solutions make law more accessible, efficient and transparent.

Our Expertise

Our mission is to bridge the gap between Law and Technology, making legal processes smarter, faster, accessible and more transparent.

Legal Document Summarization

Turning lengthy judgments and statutes into concise summaries.

Legal Text Simplification

Making complex legal language more understandable.

Rhetorical Role Labelling

Identifying the function of each sentence in a judgment (facts, arguments, precedents, decision, etc.) for structured analysis.

Statute Identification

Automatically detecting and linking relevant statutory provisions.

Legal Question Answering

Assistants for accurate responses to legal queries.

Case Law Retrieval

Intelligent and fast access to relevant precedents.
